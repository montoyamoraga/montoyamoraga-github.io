aarón montoya-moraga (1989 - present) is an artist and electrical engineer that does software for audiovisual installations, live performance, sound recording, and web. he is one half of va la paloma, a noise multimedia duo based in santiago de chile, and part of the posternura art collective. he has worked for performers maría josé contreras lorenzini and has done english to spanish translation for the processing foundation and critter & guitari. he is currently a graduate candidate at tisch school of the arts at nyu, at the interactive telecommunications program, funded by conicyt, the chilean ministry of education scholarship program.
