en la medida de lo posible

multimedia duo

about

en la medida de lo posible is a multimedia duo dealing in the intersection between performance, video and sound art.

en la medida de lo posible's performances feature motion sensors, live sound manipulation, interactive installations.

en la medida de lo posible is andrea pelegri and aaron montoya-moraga, based on both paris and brooklyn.
