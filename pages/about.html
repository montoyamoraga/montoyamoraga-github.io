bio

aarón montoya-moraga is an artist and electrical engineer that does software for audiovisual installations, live performance, sound recording, dance pieces, and interactive websites.

he is one half of va la paloma, a noise multimedia duo based in santiago de chile, and part of the posternura art collective. he performs solo as víctor raúl y sus maquinitas, a one-man audiovisual band.

he has worked for the processing foundation, critter & guitari, contemponet and maría josé contreras lorenzini.

he is currently a master's candidate from the interactive telecommunications program, tisch school of the arts, new york university, on a grant from the chilean ministry of education scholarship.
