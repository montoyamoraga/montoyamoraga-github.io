about

víctor raúl is a sound/video art project.

víctor raúl's performances feature loopers, samplers, acoustic instruments and live video and sound manipulation.

víctor raúl is a solo project by aarón montoya-moraga.

víctor raúl is a member of the multimedia artists collective posternura.
